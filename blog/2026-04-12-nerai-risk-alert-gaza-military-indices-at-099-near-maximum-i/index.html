What the Data Shows

Both are moving together, which rules out the scenario where diplomatic signalling or posturing is inflating escalation readings without corresponding physical events.

One week ago, Gaza's indices were in the 0.14–0.19 range — elevated, but not extraordinary. Today they are at the ceiling.
